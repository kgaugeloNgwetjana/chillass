About us

Chillas Entertainment HN (NPC Organization with Registration number: 2020/214117/08) is an events company dedicated to organizing and managing events, with a particular focus on the annual "Mr and Miss Hammanskraal Beauty Pageant." Our mission is to empower and motivate youth from Hammanskraal, Gauteng, South Africa, by highlighting their talents, beauty, and personal achievements while giving back to the community..
